Smashed Potatoes

Crisp and golden-brown on the outside, soft and creamy in the middle, these crispy smashed potatoes are the perfect side dish for your dinner table.

Ingredients

  • Baby Potatoes
  • Kosher Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Granulated garlic
  • Fresh herbs

Steps

  1. Boil the baby potatoes
  2. Drain them and let them dry.
  3. Lay them on an oiled baking sheet.
  4. Smash the potatoes using the bottom of a glass to gently press down.
  5. Brush the smashed potatoes with olive oil, and finish them with salt and garlic.
  6. Bake at 450 degrees Fahrenheit for 25 minutes.
  7. Take out of the oven and enjoy!
